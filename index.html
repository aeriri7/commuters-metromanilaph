Detailed Route Guide - Research Data

Based on actual field research and commuter surveys

Manila ↔ Makati

Field Tested

Transport Modes:

  • LRT-1: All Manila stations to Gil Puyat (Buendia)
  • City Bus/Jeepney: Alternative route
  • Taxi/Ride-hailing: Direct option

Travel Times:

LRT-1: 15-25 min Peak hours: +10-15 min

Fare Ranges:

  • LRT-1: ₱20-50 (₱10-25 for students)
  • City Bus: ~₱25-40
  • Taxi: ₱150-250

Most Efficient:

Manila → Makati: LRT-1 from any Manila station → walk/jeepney to final destination in Makati (Gil Puyat station is also nearby in Makati)
Makati → Manila: LRT-1 Gil Puyat station (Recommended) → walk/jeepney to final destination in Manila

Quezon City ↔ Makati

Field Tested

Transport Modes:

  • MRT-3: North Avenue to Ayala Station
  • City Bus/Jeepney: Alternative route
  • Taxi/Ride-hailing: Direct option

Travel Times:

Peak: 30-45 min Off-peak: 20-30 min Taxi: ~38-40 min

Fare Ranges:

  • MRT-3: ₱20-22
  • City Bus: ~₱70 (adult)
  • Taxi/Uber: ₱210-262

Most Efficient:

MRT-3 (QC → Ayala) + walk/jeepney to Makati CBD

BGC ↔ Manila

Field Tested

Transport Modes:

  • BGC Bus: Point-to-point & circular routes
  • Jeepneys: Along C5 corridor
  • Taxi/Uber: Direct service

Travel Times:

Standard: 20-30 min BGC Bus to Pasay/Ayala: ~30 min

Fare Ranges:

  • BGC Bus: ₱15 (flat rate)
  • City Bus: ₱15 (first 5km) + ₱2.25/km

Most Efficient:

BGC Bus (East/West route) → EDSA Ayala → jeepney/bus to Manila

Ortigas ↔ Alabang

Field Tested

Transport Modes:

  • MRT-3: Ortigas to Ayala/Magallanes
  • P2P Bus: From Ayala/Ortigas to Alabang
  • PNR: Metro Commuter Train

Travel Times:

MRT (Ortigas → Ayala): 10-15 min Total Trip: 1h 45min - 1h 50min

Fare Ranges:

  • MRT-3 segment: ~₱20
  • Bus (Ayala → Alabang): ₱49.50
  • Combined total: ~₱70
  • Direct P2P: ₱140

Most Efficient:

MRT-3 (Ortigas → Ayala) + P2P bus to Alabang (~₱70 total)

Fairview ↔ BGC

Field Tested

Transport Modes:

  • Multi-modal: Jeepney/bus → MRT → BGC Bus
  • EDSA Carousel: Alternative to MRT
  • Direct Nova Bus: Via C5 (infrequent)

Travel Times:

Heavy Traffic: 2-3 hours Via Commonwealth → C5

Fare Ranges:

  • Bus/Jeepney to MRT: ₱20-40
  • Carousel/MRT: ₱20
  • BGC Bus: ₱15
  • Total: ₱60-70
  • Direct Nova Bus: ~₱70

Most Efficient:

SM Fairview → MRT/Carousel → Guadalupe/Ayala → BGC Bus

Commuting Tips & Tricks

Peak Hours to Avoid

Morning: 7:00-9:00 AM
Evening: 5:00-8:00 PM

Travel during off-peak hours for faster and more comfortable journeys.

Payment Methods

Beep Card for MRT/LRT
Exact fare for jeepneys and buses

Always carry small bills and coins for public transport.

Safety Tips

Keep valuables secure
Be aware of your surroundings

Avoid displaying expensive items and stay alert during your journey.

Useful Apps

Sakay.ph for route planning
Grab for ride-hailing

Download transport apps to help plan your journey efficiently.

Navigation Tips

Learn major landmarks
Ask locals for directions

Familiarize yourself with key landmarks to navigate more easily.

Weather Preparedness

Bring umbrella during rainy season
Stay hydrated in summer

Check weather conditions and prepare accordingly for your commute.

Money-Saving Tips

Use Beep Card for discounts
Combine transport modes wisely

Plan multi-modal trips to save money and time on your daily commute.

Route Optimization

Check real-time traffic updates
Have backup route options

Always plan alternative routes to avoid unexpected delays and closures.

Commuter Etiquette

Give priority seats to elderly/PWD
Keep backpack in front

Be courteous to fellow commuters for a better travel experience for everyone.
